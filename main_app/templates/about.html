About Assemblr

Assemblr is an app that brings together UX Designers & Sofware Engineers from General Assembly programs. Our goal is to connect students who are looking for help on a project or who simply want to lend a helping hand to a fellow GA student. If you need help on a project, simply create a post so others can respond.
Assemblr also aims to help students beyond their experience at General Assembly. By providing access to a database of freelance job opportunities, Assemblr helps graduates obtain experience while they continue their job search.
