About Assemblr

Assemblr is an app that brings together UX Designers & Sofware Engineers from General Assembly programs. Our goal is to connect students who are looking for help on a project or who simply want to lend a helping hand to a fellow GA student.

Assemblr also aims to help students beyond their experience at General Assembly. By providing access to a database of freelance job opportunities, Assemblr helps graduates obtain experience while students continue their job search.
